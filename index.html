Tips and examples

\
Escapes a special character—www\.qld\.gov\.au\/ matches www.qld.gov.au/
.
Matches any letter, number or symbol—www.qld.gov.au matches www*qld*gov*au
.+
Matches one or more letters, numbers or symbols
.*
Matches zero or more letters, numbers or symbols
s?
Matches an optional s—https? matches http and https
